ProtonVerse: a family friendly non-profit platform

Join for free

ProtonVerse is a family friendly metaverse with assets secured on Bitcoin blockchain. Using VR (virtual reality) AR (augmented reality) and spacial computing technologies, together with state of the art game creation tools and Web3 payments platform, users will construct virtual spaces for people to meet; connect with friends; to build, buy, sell and rent virtual property and run their own businesses within the ProtonVerse.

Opportunities for real-time interactions by audio, text, avatar, motion capture or other means will be provided but the choice whether or not to engage will be up to the individual or group.
